Management Team

   Yuki Hiraki

Co-founder

A serial entrepreneur, Yuki embarked on EnerScale after selling his previous business. Prior to EnerScale, Yuki co-founded LCO-Creation, a Singapore-based B2B Mobile backend as a service (MBaaS)/ Big Data startup focusing on Location-Based Technologies. As the Co-Founder & CEO, Yuki led both management and technology development, oversaw operations in Singapore, China, Vietnam, and Japan, and accomplished the company’s further global expansion. After completing share transfers and strategic investment in 2015, LCO became a group company of a publicly listed IT & Marketing firm. Prior to LCO, Yuki was a project manager at NTT Communications where he led VPN development projects for multinational corporations. Before joining NTT, he was involved in Japan's Smart City research granted by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology (research topic: optimization modeling of environmental impact and costs associated with Solar PVs). In 2019, Yuki was selected as a member of Singapore Business Federation’s Young Business Leaders Network . He holds a master's degree in Science and Engineering from Chuo University in Japan.

   Saki Kobayashi

Co-founder

A serial entrepreneur, Saki embarked on EnerScale after selling her previous business. Prior to EnerScale, Saki co-founded LCO-Creation, a Singapore-based B2B Mobile backend as a service (MBaaS)/ Big Data startup focusing on Location-Based Technologies. As the Co-Founder & COO, Saki initiated LCO’s investment from the Singapore Government’s National Research Foundation, led business development, and built strategic partnerships across the globe. Prior to co-founding LCO, she helped with overseas expansion at NTT Communications. She is a frequent speaker and moderator at international conferences and has served as a mentor and judge for various startup competitions and programs, including those organized by Nanyang Business School, Tokyo Metropolitan Government’s APT Women (Acceleration Program in Tokyo for Women) Singapore Dispatch Program, among others. Saki was selected as a member of Singapore Business Federation’s Young Business Leaders Network and in 2020, was named among the “100 Inspiring Women Travel Founders” by TravelMassive. She holds a B.A. in International Liberal Studies from Waseda University in Japan.

Yang Li

CTO

Yang became the CTO of EnerScale in 2021. Prior to EnerScale, Yang was a full-stack engineer, and eventually the CTO of LCO, where he managed development teams in China and Vietnam. At LCO, Yang played a key role in developing OEM apps, mBaaS platforms, and Big Data analytics/AI modeling. A former Baidu engineer, Yang led various AI projects in Beijing and Shenzhen. He was also a development manager for Baidu’s popular Japanese keyboard app “Simeji.” He holds a Master’s Degree in Engineering from Keio University in Japan.
