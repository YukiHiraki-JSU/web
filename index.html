About Us


EnerScale was founded with a mission to Scaling-up Clean Energy Access across the globe. Our next-generation Energy Data Intelligence Platform, EnerScale, helps businesses and organizations in the off-grid energy sector deliver more affordable, reliable, sustainable, and clean energy to unelectrified and under-electrified populations across the world. We envision a world where all people, regardless of their socioeconomic backgrounds, can thrive and unlock their full potential through access to electricity. By accelerating the clean energy revolution through the power of data and business intelligence, EnerScale is committed to closing the gap in healthcare, education, and information technology.

Challenges


Today, nearly 800 million people worldwide still lack access to electricity. On top of this, close to 1 billion people only have access to unreliable energy sources. This means, about 2 billion people live without reliable and uninterrupted energy supplies. Off-grid solutions, such as mini-grids and solar home systems (SHSs), play important roles in rural electrification and are gaining momentum in the solar energy sector. However, electrification is not an end in itself. Access to reliable, affordable, and clean energy resources is crucial for sustainable economic growth, and the lack of it could hinder economic growth.
